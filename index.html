The PawPack difference

One retired climbing rope. A safety-rated material trusted with human lives, given a second purpose on the trail. The provenance isn't a story we tell — it's the object in your hand.

100% Retired Climbing Rope

Sourced directly from gyms and guide services when rope retires from climbing service — then rebuilt for trail use.

Fully Traceable Provenance

Every leash ships with the gym of origin, rope model, and retirement date. Verifiable, not marketed.

Zero Waste Manufacturing

Core becomes the leash. Sheath becomes the collar. Offcuts become bandanas and treat-bag details. Nothing gets wasted.

Built for the Actual Trail

Leash lengths, handle leverage, collars, and accessories configured for movement outside — not just the sidewalk loop.

The Journey

From the wall
to the trail.

Six steps. One rope. Nothing wasted.

Gym Partnership
01.

Sourcing

Partner gyms and guide services hand off retired rope that has completed its climbing life. We verify spec, origin, and retirement date.

Inspection
02.

Intake

Each rope is logged, photographed, and tagged with its provenance card. The gym, the spec, the date — all travel with the rope.

Separation
03.

Sheath & Core

Sheath is stripped from core. Core becomes the leash. Sheath is cut into collar components. Offcuts are saved for bandanas and trail accessories.

Richmond, VA
04.

Cutting

Rope is cut to trail-ready lengths in our Richmond workshop. Ends are heat-finished to prevent fraying. No filler, no throwaway trim.

Hand Assembly
05.

Assembly

Hardware attached by hand. Each unit is inspected, tagged with provenance, and paired to the rest of the rope family.

Trail-Ready
06.

To Your Door

Shipped in a compostable box with a handwritten note and the story of the rope’s first life — ready for its second.
